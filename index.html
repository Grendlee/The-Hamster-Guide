Grant's Guide

Habitat

A hamster shoud have ample living space. The living area should not be a cage but a large pastic bin. Cages can cause stress for the hamster. Ample digger material is also required. As well as sand for bathing.

Diet

Hamsters eat veggies, seed mix, bugs. Provide the hamster with a balanced diet by rotatin veggies with seed mix. Provide bugs every other day.

Handling

Hamsters are very fragile creatures. Handle with care. They often do not like to be touched.

Behaviour

Hamstesr are awake during dawn and dusk. Make sure their living area is quiet and clean.

Seeing A Vet

It is recomended to see a vet every 6 months. The vet can perform xrays to know if there are any cancers or unknown issues.

Should You Get A Hamster?

Hamsters are difficult animals to take care of. They are not natural pack animals like cats or dogs. Providing a heallthy life for a hamster takes a lot of effort and time. Often hamster owners dont know they are hurting the animal.
